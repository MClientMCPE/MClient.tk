MClient++ for MCPE

Discord Server

A Minecraft Pocket Edition Hack Client for Android

MClient is a public hack client (recoded toolbox), made for android.
We have a custom GUI (ClickGUI) which has all the utility hacks of toolbox and more!
All Toolbox features that would normally be premium are free in MClient
MClient also has improved hacks such as killaura and HitboxExpand.
There are also new modules in MClient such as AirInfinity, more XRay options, and more
Make sure to join the discord and share the client!
